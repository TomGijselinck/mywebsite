About (Me and my Blog)

Hello! My name is Tom Gijselinck and I'm studying Computer Science and Electrical Engineering at the KU Leuven (Belgium). My main interests are computer security, cryptography, algorithm design, UNIX/Linux, numerical analysis and web programming.

I also participate in athletics at competition level running 800m and 1500m. One day I hope getting a medal at the national championships. My current goals are breaking the club records on the 800m and 1500m.

About this site

I consider my blog as a sort of notebook and laboratory for trying new things. It helps reminding me the solutions to interesting problems I encounter. I'll also try to use it as a portfolio of my personal and academic projects and accomplishments.

This website is built on: Django, SQLite, Apache, Nginx, Linode, Bootstrap framework, Ubuntu, mod_wsgi, Python and edited with Vim running in tmux. This website is running in a Docker container. You can browse the source code of this website on my GitHub page.
