About (Me and my Blog)

Hello! My name is Tom Gijselinck and I'm a studying Computer Science and Electrical Engineering at the KU Leuven (Belgium). My main interests are computer security, cryptography, algorithm design, numerical analysis and web programming.

I also participate in athletics at competition level running 800m and 1500m. One day I hope getting a medal at the national championships. My current goals are breaking the club records on the 800m and 1500m.

I consider my blog as a sort of neat scratchbook and laboratory for trying new things. It helps reminding me the solutions to interesting problems I encounter. I'll also try to use it as a portfolio of my personal and academic projects and accomplishments.
